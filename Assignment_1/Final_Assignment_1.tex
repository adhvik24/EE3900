\section{QUESTION 2.17.a}
Give the magnitude and direction of the net force acting on a stone of mass 0.1 kg,just after it is dropped from the window of a stationary train.
Neglect air resistance.
\section{SOLUTION}
As the train is stationary, the only force acting on the mass is gravitational force which acts in the downward direction.\\
The direction of net force is $\myvec{0\\-1\\0} $.\\
And let the magnitude of net force is F.\\
Then,
\begin{align}
  \nonumber  F&=m*g\\
  \nonumber  &=0.1*10\\
 \nonumber   F&=1
\end{align}
$\therefore$ Magnitude of net force is $1$N.\\ \brak{\,Taking,\,\,g = 10ms^{-2}}
